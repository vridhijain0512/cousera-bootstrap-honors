About Us

Our History

Started in 2010, Menskart quickly established itself as a fashion icon par excellence in US. With its unique brand of world fusion fashion that can be found nowhere else, it enjoys patronage from the A-list clientele in US. Featuring four of the best three-star fashion designers in the world, you never know what will arrive on your magazine the next time you visit us.

The company traces its humble beginnings to The Cloth Company, a successful firm started by our CEO, Mr. Peter Pan, that featured for the first time in the world's best fashion magazine.
